Our Mission

Our Mission is to provide scientific and engineering information on electric power and energy for the betterment of society, and to be the preferred professional development source for our members.

Our Vision

Our vision is to set the foundation for a world running on clean energy, empowering future generations to achieve this goal.

PES’s Field of Interest

PES develops standards and drives innovation in electric power and energy — spanning generation, transmission, distribution, and utilization. It champions safe, reliable, resilient, and sustainable AC and DC electricity systems, while fostering advancements in energy resources, smart grids, and smart cities for societal benefit.

About PES

The IEEE Power & Energy Society is a worldwide, non-profit association of more than 37,000 industry professionals, academics and students with a common interest in the electric power energy industry. PES has vibrant communities at the local chapter level, and at the international level through our conferences, publications and technical committees.
